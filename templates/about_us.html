Agro Hub

Agro Hub is a QA site for professional and enthusiast farmers. It is a privately held website created in 2020 by Roshan Ojha. It features questions and answers on any topic in farming.

The website serves as a platform for users to ask and answer questions, and to buy and sell agricultural products.

Technology: HTML, CSS, Jquery and Jinja2 are used fo frontend, Flask for backend and Sqlite3 for database.
